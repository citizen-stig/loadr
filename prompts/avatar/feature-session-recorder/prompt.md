Create a polished brand illustration of the same recurring humanoid robot character with a consistent design across every image.

CHARACTER IDENTITY:
A fast, strong, stealth-capable, highly intelligent futuristic android built for software performance testing and technical analysis. The character has a broad but athletic silhouette, powerful shoulders, compact waist, articulated mechanical limbs, and layered tactical armour. It must feel capable and durable without looking like a military weapon or construction worker.

VISUAL DESIGN:

* Matte black and dark graphite armour
* Deep charcoal mechanical joints
* Bright red illuminated accents only
* One continuous narrow red visor
* Angular helmet with a smooth swept crown
* Circular mechanical modules at both sides of the head
* Large geometric shoulder armour
* Clean armoured chest plate with subtle red vents
* Segmented forearms, hands, thighs, knees, and boots
* Small integrated utility modules and concealed technical systems
* No handheld tools
* No guns, weapons, hammers, drills, spanners, or construction equipment
* No blue, cyan, green, orange, or gold
* No exposed human face
* No cape or clothing

PERSONALITY:
Calm, capable, precise, intelligent, observant, dependable, slightly intimidating but still approachable. The character should feel like a high-performance technical specialist rather than a soldier.

STYLE:
Stylised premium cartoon-meets-concept-art illustration. Slightly exaggerated proportions, simplified readable shapes, bold silhouette, crisp linework, controlled cel shading, subtle dimensional rendering, minimal surface wear, clean brand-friendly finish. More animated and expressive than photorealistic, but not cute, childish, or chibi.

POSE:
A low, alert ready stance — one knee bent, head turned tracking movement, one hand lifted to catch a stream of light — stealthy and observant.

ACTION OR CONTEXT:
Capturing live traffic — the stealth android intercepts a flow of requests and condenses it into a scenario.

EXPRESSION THROUGH BODY LANGUAGE:
Watchful, quiet, precise; poised stillness with every sense engaged.

CROP:
Full body or three-quarter to show the low, ready posture.

CAMERA:
Slight high or eye-level three-quarter.

ZOOM:
Medium-wide, with the captured stream visible.

COMPOSITION:
Character low and to one side, a ribbon of red request packets flowing into the raised hand.

BACKGROUND:
Transparent background or a clean near-white neutral background. No environment unless specifically requested. Keep the silhouette clear and isolated.

OPTIONAL DIGITAL ELEMENTS:
A flowing red holographic stream of request/response packets condensing into a small structured scenario card — motion and capture, minimal red.

CONSISTENCY REQUIREMENTS:
Preserve the exact same helmet shape, visor shape, shoulder geometry, chest armour, red accent placement, body proportions, hand design, leg armour, and overall silhouette in every image. Treat this as the same branded character in a different pose, not a redesign.

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
High-resolution commercial mascot artwork, clean edges, readable at small sizes, suitable for website sections, social posts, documentation, loading states, feature cards, avatars, and marketing graphics.
